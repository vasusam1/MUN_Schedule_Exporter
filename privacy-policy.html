Privacy Policy for MUN Schedule Exporter Chrome Extension

Last Updated: [Current Date]

1. Overview

MUN Schedule Exporter ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that allows Memorial University students to export their class schedules to ICS calendar format. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

NO DATA IS COLLECTED, STORED, OR TRANSMITTED.

The Extension operates entirely locally in your browser and does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Store data on servers
  • Transmit data to external services
  • Use tracking or analytics
  • Share data with third parties

3. How the Extension Works

When you use the Extension:

  1. You navigate to your MUN schedule page (self-service.mun.ca)
  2. You click the Extension button
  3. The Extension reads your schedule data from the webpage
  4. An ICS file is generated locally on your computer
  5. All data is immediately discarded - nothing is retained

4. Permissions Explanation

The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: Temporarily accesses the current tab to read your MUN schedule page. Only used when you click the Extension button.
  • scripting: Injects a minimal script to parse the schedule table. Only runs on MUN schedule pages when triggered by you.
  • https://self-service.mun.ca/*: Required to access Memorial University's official schedule portal. The Extension only works on this specific website.

5. Data Processing Location

All data processing occurs locally on your device. No information leaves your browser.

6. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external resources.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is intended for Memorial University students and is not directed at children under 13.
